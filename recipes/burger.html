The Perfect Basic Burger

Information

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Total Time: 20 mins
  • Yield: 4 burger patties
  • Cook time: 15 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs
